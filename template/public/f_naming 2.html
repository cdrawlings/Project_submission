Note: an existing product is not renamed for an added feature or enhancement. This includes:

  • New development to an existing product
  • Adding new functionality to an existing product
  • Adding a new, chargeable feature or enhancement to an existing product

The process: The brand team begins the naming process by determining if your element is

  • A platform capability, can it connect to or is it consumed by a product and/or feature?
  • A platform setting, does it allow capabilities of the platform to be tailored?
  • A product, does it facilitate the consumption of the platform capabilities?
  • A service, is it a self-serve or human interaction component that is shared across the organization and/or our customer base?
  • A feature, is it a distinguishing characteristic or element of a product or capability that performs a function?
